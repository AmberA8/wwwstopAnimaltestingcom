Brands That Don't & Do Animal Testing.

Unfortunately there are many brands that do animal testing. Here are popular brands you could be using. And other brands which are cruelty-free. Buying from only brands that are cruelty-free greatly helps against animal testing.

Brands that do Animal Testing.

    Chanel


    This famous luxury brand sadly isn't cruelty-free. Chanel sells their products worldwide, including in China where it is mandatory by law.

    L'Oreal


Although many believe that L'Oreal is cruelty-free. LOreal sells products in China.

    CeraVe


Although CeraVe claims to be cruelty-free, the L'Oreal group owns the brand, which does animal testing.

Brands that are cruelty free


    Denttabs

Denttabs are a toothpaste brand. Their toothpaste is 100% free from plastic, preservatives, etc. They also prevent space for plaque to form.


    AG Hair


    AG is a cruelty free brand with a wide variety of hair products.
